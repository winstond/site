पेश (pesh)

adjective/verb -- Urdu origin; formal
1.
presented, offered (synonyms: प्रस्तुत (Sanskrit))
Frequency: high
Cultural Note: Urdu-origin word used with करना (to present) or होना (to appear). Common in formal contexts.
Common Mistakes: Using as standalone verb; incorrect collocation
उसने अपनी रिपोर्ट पेश की। She presented her report.
(usane apanee riport pesh kee।)
Register: Neutral * Tense: Past
कल बजट पेश होगा। The budget will be presented tomorrow.
(kal bajat pesh hogaa।)
Register: Formal * Tense: Future
मंत्री ने नया विधेयक पेश किया। The minister presented a new bill.
(mantree ne nayaa vidheyak pesh kiyaa।)
Register: Formal * Tense: Past
कृपया अपना परिचय पेश करें। Please present your introduction.
(kripayaa apanaa parichay pesh karen।)
Register: Formal * Tense: Present (imperative)
गवाह को अदालत में पेश किया गया। The witness was presented in court.
(gavaah ko adaalat men pesh kiyaa gayaa।)
Register: Formal * Tense: Past
उन्होंने सबूत पेश किए। They presented evidence.
(unhonne saboot pesh kie।)
Register: Neutral * Tense: Past
2.
facing, confronted with (synonyms: सामना)
Frequency: medium
Cultural Note: Used in पेश आना (to encounter, to behave toward). Idiomatic usage.
Common Mistakes: Confusing with पेश करना; missing आना collocation
ऐसी समस्या पहले कभी पेश नहीं आई। Such a problem has never been encountered before.
(aisee samasyaa pahale kabhee pesh naheen aaee।)
Register: Neutral * Tense: Past
जो भी मुश्किल पेश आए, हम तैयार हैं। Whatever difficulty arises, we are ready.
(jo bhee mushkil pesh aae, ham taiyaar hain।)
Register: Neutral * Tense: Present (subjunctive)
उसके साथ बुरा पेश आया। He was treated badly.
(usake saath buraa pesh aayaa।)
Register: Neutral * Tense: Past
मेहमानों से अच्छे पेश आओ। Behave well with guests.
(mehamaanon se achchhe pesh aao।)
Register: Neutral * Tense: Present (imperative)

Morphology

पेश -- invariant: पेश (pesh) -- Urdu origin; used with करना/होना/आना

रवानी से (ravaani se)

adverb -- compound adverb; manner
1.
fluently, smoothly (synonyms: धाराप्रवाह (formal), फ़र्राटे से)
Frequency: medium
Cultural Note: Describes smooth, uninterrupted flow, especially of speech or language. From Urdu रवानी (flow).
Common Mistakes: Confusing with तेज़ी से (quickly); using for speed rather than smoothness
वह रवानी से हिंदी बोलता है। He speaks Hindi fluently.
(vah ravaanee se hindee bolataa hai।)
Register: Neutral * Tense: Present (habitual)
उसने रवानी से भाषण दिया। She gave a speech fluently.
(usane ravaanee se bhaashan diyaa।)
Register: Neutral * Tense: Past
मैं रवानी से पढ़ना सीखूँगा। I will learn to read fluently.
(main ravaanee se parhanaa seekhoongaa।)
Register: Neutral * Tense: Future
बच्चा अभी रवानी से नहीं पढ़ पाता। The child can't read fluently yet.
(bachchaa abhee ravaanee se naheen parh paataa।)
Register: Neutral * Tense: Present
रवानी से बोलने में समय लगता है। It takes time to speak fluently.
(ravaanee se bolane men samay lagataa hai।)
Register: Neutral * Tense: Present

Morphology

रवानी से -- invariant: रवानी से (ravaani se) -- compound adverb
