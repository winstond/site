जब तक (jab tak)

conjunction -- temporal; until/as long as
1.
until, till (synonyms: जब तक कि)
Frequency: high
Cultural Note: Used to mark endpoint in time. Often paired with तब तक. In Hindi, 'until X happens' often uses जब तक X नहीं होता.
Common Mistakes: Confusing with जब (when); forgetting negative in 'until not' constructions
जब तक वह नहीं आता, हम इंतज़ार करेंगे। We will wait until he comes.
(jab tak vah naheen aataa, ham intazaar karenge।)
