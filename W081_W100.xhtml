आज रात को (aaj raat ko)

adverb -- compound adverb; temporal
1.
tonight, this night (synonyms: आज रात, इस रात)
Frequency: high
Cultural Note: Common temporal expression. को adds specificity. Very natural in spoken Hindi.
Common Mistakes: Omitting को; confusing with कल रात (last/tomorrow night)
आज रात को मैं नहीं आऊँगा। I will not come tonight.
(aaj raat ko main naheen aaoongaa।)

अवश्य (avashya)

adverb -- formal; certainly
1.
certainly, definitely, surely (synonyms: ज़रूर (casual), निश्चित रूप से (formal))
Frequency: high
Cultural Note: Formal/literary adverb. Common in written Hindi, news, official contexts.
Common Mistakes: Using in casual speech where ज़रूर is more natural

(main avashy aaoongaa।)
