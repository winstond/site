क्योंकि (kyonki)

conjunction -- causal; because
1.
because, since (synonyms: चूँकि (formal), इसलिए कि)
Frequency: high
Cultural Note: The most common causal conjunction in spoken Hindi. Usually introduces the reason clause. Often paired with इसलिए in result clause.
Common Mistakes: Placing cause before effect without इसलिए; confusing with चूँकि in register
मैं नहीं आया क्योंकि मैं बीमार था। I didn't come because I was sick.
(main naheen aayaa kyonki main beemaar thaa।)
